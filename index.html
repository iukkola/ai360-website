Primary Focus: Sport & AI

Understanding sport beyond scores

Sport is the core domain of AI360. We develop AI-assisted concepts that support:

  • Match understanding and tactical explanation
  • Real-time and post-event commentary assistance
  • Educational and interactive sport experiences

Football is our primary sport. The same principles apply to other structured team sports, and in later phases may extend to areas such as American football, rugby, and baseball.

Secondary Focus: Carbon Removal & Measurement

Alongside sport, AI360 explores how applied AI can support carbon removal and carbon credit measurement, particularly in agriculture.

  • Practical data collection workflows
  • Evidence-based monitoring over time
  • Improving transparency and trust in measurement processes

This is a secondary research and pilot track.
